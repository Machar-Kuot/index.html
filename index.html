About Nuru Basketball Club

Nuru Basketball Club was founded in 2018 with a mission to develop young talent, promote teamwork, and bring the love of basketball to the community in Nairobi.

We believe basketball is more than just a sport — it's a tool for discipline, leadership, and personal growth.
