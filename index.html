About Us

At Kings Sports, we are building the leading basketball brand in Africa. We envision a future where basketball transcends boundaries and African talent shines on the global stage.

Our journey is fueled by an intense passion for the game, and our commitment extends beyond the court. We are actively amplifying the individual, social, and economic impact of youth sports.

We recognize and leverage trends, gaps, and opportunities within the basketball community, and use our convening power to promote African basketball by developing professional teams, promoting youth basketball, and establishing sports facilities.

02

Tiger Basketball Club

The First South Sudan team to qualify for the Basketball Africa League (BAL).

2× Unbeaten champions, South Sudan Basketball Federation Premier League
2024–25 First South Sudan team to qualify for the BAL
#WeAreTigers All in for the fans

We made history in 2024 and 2025 by being the first South Sudan team to qualify for the BAL. We remained undefeated in two straight seasons (2023–2024 & 2024–2025).

We are more than a winning basketball brand — we are on a mission to transform the landscape of the sport on the continent.

Nurturing Talent And Fostering A Love for the Game

Kings Initiatives

Kings Sports is giving back to the community. We bring professional-level coaching and resources to their doorstep. Through youth development programs, we're committed to nurturing the next generation of South Sudan basketball stars.

Kings Men Street League Team

We build champions from the asphalt up. Our street league teams cultivate raw talent. In our first year we took home the Street League Championship!

Kings Women Street League Team

We are committed to providing equal opportunities for young women. Our women's team qualified for playoffs and is gearing up to top the league.

Court Rehabilitation & Community Clinics

We build and rehabilitate courts while running clinics in schools across the region. We also partner with Deementor to support young Ethiopian refugees through sports and education.
