About Our Club

Welcome to Nuru Basketball Club, the premier hub for elite basketball training, community development, and competitive sportsmanship. Founded with a vision to nurture raw talent and build character through the game of basketball, our club provides an environment where athletes of all ages can thrive both on and off the court.

Whether you are a beginner looking to master the fundamentals of dribbling and shooting, or an advanced player aspiring to compete at the collegiate and professional levels, Nuru Basketball Club offers world-class coaching to help you reach your maximum potential.

How to Join the Club

If you want to become a part of the Nuru Basketball Club family, follow these mandatory steps:

  1. Download and fill out the online registration form.
  2. Submit your physical sports clearance form from a certified doctor.
  3. Attend the open tryouts held every Saturday morning at 8:00 AM.
  4. Review the team handbook and accept the club code of conduct.
  5. Pay the seasonal kit and training registration fee.
