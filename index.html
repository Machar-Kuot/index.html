⚡ Cobra Sports · Hoops Legacy

Cobra Sports is a dynamic community organization rooted in passion for basketball, youth development, and African excellence. Under its flagship, Nara Basketball Club (NBC) has become a powerhouse — the very first team in history to qualify for the prestigious Basketball African League from our region. We unite players, coaches, and fans across generations, building champions on and off the court.

From dusty courts to continental glory, Cobra Sports empowers grassroots talent and creates opportunities. Nara BC is the heartbeat of our mission, breaking barriers and flying the flag high in the BAL.

  4. ✈️ 2024 – Historic debut in Basketball African League
