Our Journey to the Basketball African League

  1. Founded in 2018 as a community basketball initiative under Cobra Sports
  2. Recruited top local talent from across the region in 2019
  3. Won the Regional Championship in 2020
  4. Secured national league promotion in 2021
  5. Reached the National Finals in 2022
  6. Qualified for the Basketball African League in 2023 — making history as the first team!

What We Offer

  • Professional coaching for all age groups
  • State-of-the-art training facilities
  • Youth development programs
  • Community outreach and basketball clinics
  • Competitive league participation
  • Scholarship opportunities for talented players
  • Fitness and nutrition programs
